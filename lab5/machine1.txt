<Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse> is a 2018 animated superhero film featuring the Marvel Comics character Miles Morales, produced by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Animation.
Directed by Bob Persichetti, Peter Ramsey, and Rodney Rothman, <Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse> stars Shameik Moore as Morales, alongside Jake Johnson, Hailee Steinfeld, and others.
<Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse>, inspired by the "Spider-Verse" story arc, follows Miles as he becomes the new Spider-Man and joins other Spider-People from various parallel universes to save his universe from Kingpin.
<Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse> was the first animated feature in the Spider-Man franchise and received widespread acclaim for its innovative animation style and storytelling.
<Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse> won the Best Animated Feature at the 91st Academy Awards, among other accolades.
Following the success of <Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse>, a sequel, <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse>, was released in 2023, and further expansions like <Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse> and a spin-off are in development.
