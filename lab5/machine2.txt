<Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse>, a 2023 animated superhero film, is the sequel to <Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse>.
Produced by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Animation, <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse> features the Marvel Comics character Miles Morales.
Directed by Joaquim Dos Santos, Kemp Powers, and Justin K. Thompson, in <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse> Shameik Moore returns as Miles Morales, joining a cast including Hailee Steinfeld and Brian Tyree Henry.
<Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse> showcases Miles' journey across multiple universes, encountering a team of Spider-People and facing a new antagonist, the Spot.
The development of <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse> began prior to the release of <Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse>, focusing on the dynamic between Miles and Gwen Stacy.
The premiere of <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse> took place in Los Angeles in May 2023, with its U.S. release in June.
<Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse>, like its predecessor, was critically praised and achieved significant box office success, grossing $690.5 million worldwide.
Following the success of <Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse>, the third installment, <Spider-Man: Beyond the Spider-Verse> is in development, along with a spinoff.
